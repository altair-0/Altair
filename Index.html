About Altair Ibn La Ahad

Altair Ibn La Ahad is the iconic protagonist of the Assassin's Creed video game series. He is a skilled member of the Levantine Brotherhood of Assassins during the Third Crusade. Known for his agility, stealth, and determination, Altair's story has captivated gamers worldwide.

Skills

  • Master of parkour and free-running
  • Proficient in various weapons, especially the hidden blade
  • Expert in stealth and assassination techniques
  • Skilled in hand-to-hand combat

Story

Altair's journey is filled with danger, self-discovery, and the pursuit of justice. From his humble beginnings in Masyaf to his encounters with historical figures, his actions shape the Assassin-Templar conflict. Experience the thrilling narrative of Altair as he uncovers the secrets of his order.
